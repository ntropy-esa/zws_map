About the project

This map was set up by Zero Waste Stockholm to help citizens consume differently, with less packaging. Data are for now limited to Stockholm area, but anyone in Sweden is welcome to contribute. The map is based on the work of CartoVrac.

Current contributors to the mapping of shops:

  • © OpenStreetMap contributors
  • You, via this form
  • Zero Waste Stockholm, and its members

Shop owners are also welcome to share their information with us, or to reference themselves on © OpenStreetMap.

How it works

This map only displays data from from OpenStreetMap https://openstreetmap.org).

To be visible on this map, shops must be referenced on OpenStreetMap and tagged with bulk purchase.

Thank you CartoVrac!

CartoVrac is a French team that developed this mapping tool and made it available for free. A big thank you to them for sharing and helping us out in setting up the Swedish map.

To follow or contribute to the development of the project, head to GitHub and its Wiki.
